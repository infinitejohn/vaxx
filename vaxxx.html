Frequently Asked Questions

How does your real-time stock verification work?
We integrate inventory feeds, barcode/RFID checks and IoT sensors to reconcile stock and produce live verification dashboards.
What kind of analytics do you provide?
Demand forecasting, wastage risk scoring, uptake trends, and supply chain KPIs — all powered by explainable AI models.
How does your equipment monitoring work?
We ingest telemetry from temperature sensors & gateways; anomalies raise automated alerts and corrective workflows.
Can you customize your solutions to meet our specific needs?
Yes. We provide custom integrations, bespoke dashboards and SLA-backed enterprise deployments for large partners.
What is your approach to patient privacy and data security?
We follow privacy-by-design, encrypt data in transit and at rest, and operate with compliance standards such as GDPR/HIPAA where required.
